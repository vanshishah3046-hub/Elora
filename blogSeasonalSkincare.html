Seasonal Skincare Guide

By Elora Skincare · September 18, 2025

Your skin’s needs shift with every season. From summer’s intense sun to winter’s dry air, adjusting your routine keeps your complexion balanced year-round.

Spring: Refresh & Renew

Use gentle exfoliants to remove winter buildup and reveal brighter skin. Lightweight hydrating serums keep oil and dryness in check.

Summer: Sun Protection First

Daily SPF 30+ is non-negotiable—reapply every two hours when outdoors.

Opt for oil-free moisturizers and antioxidant-rich serums to combat free radical damage.

Autumn: Rebuild the Barrier

Transition to richer creams and incorporate ceramide-packed treatments to strengthen the skin barrier as the air cools.

Winter: Deep Hydration

  • Use a humidifier to fight indoor dryness.
  • Layer hydrating toner, serum, and a thick night cream.
  • Avoid overly hot showers to prevent moisture loss.

By rotating your routine with the seasons, you’ll maintain a healthy, radiant complexion no matter the weather.
